Our websites are designed for results

We know what it takes to develop successful and effective websites.

Adaptability

Your customers should be comfortable viewing and using your website from any device. Our responsive websites work great on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, as they are all adapting to any screen size.

Usability

As a final result, the user of your website would has to become your client, and for this he requires our assistance. We develop sites that are useful to users and easy to use, so that visitors are more likely to become your customers.

Copywriting

An effective website that converts users and sales is more than just a beautiful website. The website should be useful to the user and be clearer than of your competitors. For our websites we write content that search engines like and attract customers.

Content management

We are always ready to help you with website maintenance. But if you want to make content updating by you own, it’s easy! We integrate simple in use CMS (content management system) to all our websites and teach our clients how easily to edit and add content.

SEO

Your website needs an audience that can become your customers. We will make sure that your website is optimized for SEO having implemented advanced search engine optimization experience, that it could be ready for further promotion.

Photos

A good photo can sell for you, that’s why to pick a right photo is a vital thing for representing your business and is an important part of each project. We can help you to be sure, that visual effects on your website are really reflecting your brand and your offer.

Hosting and maintenance

We can take care of hosting your website on ourselves. In addition, we will also maintain your website so that it can be always up-to-date and fast and receives all the latest security updates.

Website Analytics

Website analytics allows you to find out what users like or don't like, visiting your site. By knowing your visitor’s interests, we can increase conversion rate and your company's sales. At MangoBloom Solutions, we implement Analytics on all our sites.

Project maintenance

Are you planning to develop your project throughout the project life cycle? Our team is always ready to help you with this. We use flexible methodologies for project management, that allow us to deal with your project without stopping.
